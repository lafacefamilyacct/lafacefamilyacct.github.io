Welcome to the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute Data Sciences Computing Site

The Data Sciences Systems Group is responsible for the planning, implementation and support of all departmental Windows, Mac and Unix servers, PC and Mac desktops, and mobile computing for DS staff.  Our “core services” include: application software license maintenance; technology hardware acquisition, maintenance and upgrading; OS upgrades; networking; data center operations; general user and application support; email and web services and data backup and restoring.

Regular Hours for System Support are:

Monday – Friday, 8:00AM – 5:30PM

Please email systems@ds.dfci.harvard.edu

with any computing-related questions or problems.
